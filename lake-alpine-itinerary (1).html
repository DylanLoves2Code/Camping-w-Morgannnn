Day 2 — Inspiration Point

Saturday, Jul 25
Morning

Coffee, Breakfast & Move Camp

Slow start at camp, then break down Site #1 and re-set at Site #8 for the second night. Fuel up before the hike — it's a steep one, even though it's short.

Mid-Morning

Hike to Inspiration Point

An out-and-back trail along the Lakeshore Trail to the Inspiration Point spur — about 2.5 miles round trip with ~600 ft of elevation gain, roughly 1.5–2 hours including time at the top. Rocky and steep near the summit, but the payoff is a panoramic view over Lake Alpine, Mount Reba, and the Dardanelles beyond.

~2.5 mi round trip ~600 ft gain Moderate
Plan BIf the trail's crowded, wet, or anyone's not up for the climb: the Lakeshore Trail loops the full lake in about 4 easy, mostly flat miles — same views of the water, none of the scramble.
Afternoon

Lake Time

Cool off back at Lake Alpine — swim, fish, or just sit on the shore and recover from the climb.

Evening

Camp Dinner & Fire — Site #8

Linguiça on the fire, sandwiches if anyone's still hungry, and s'mores once it's dark. Second and final night under the pines.
